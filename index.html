About This Collection

Starting slowly in 2024 after discovering the Upper Deck Blizzard Legacy Collection set from 2023 — even the base cards of Warcraft were enough to get me excited — my passion for building a collection only grew from there. Joining eBay, COMC and Upper Deck E-Pack, I found many ways to dive deeper into the hobby whenever the opportunity arose.

What I love most about collecting cards is that it's a never-ending marathon — and that's exactly why I enjoy waiting for the right deals at the right price. There's no need to rush.

Enjoy!
